05 — Why DAYDA

WHY
DAY
DA?

We started DAYDA because we kept asking ourselves the same question: why does dancing have to happen at 2AM?

There's this feeling, you know it, when a song hits while you're walking down the street and your whole body wants to move. That urge is real, and it's telling you something. Dancing makes you happier. It pulls you out of your head. It connects you to the people around you.

But somewhere along the way, we decided that dancing belongs to the night. To the hangover. To the Tuesday you spend half-dead at your desk. We think that's a shame.

So we took the clubs, the real ones, with proper floors and proper sound, and we opened them up during the day. Short sessions. Good music. People who showed up for the same reason you did. And as a bonus, we get to keep these spaces alive in a city that needs them.

That's DAYDA. Come dance with us, while the sun is still up.
